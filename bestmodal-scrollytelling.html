Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about how BestModal works, what it covers and how to get started.

Request a Demo
Which transport modes can be managed in BestModal?
BestModal supports all major transport modes: Ocean (FCL and LCL), Road (FTL and LTL), Rail, Barge and Airfreight. All modes are managed under one unified pricing logic, including their mode-specific surcharges, local charges and combination scenarios.
Does BestModal calculate only tariffs or complete prices?
BestModal calculates complete transport prices — not just base tariffs. Every price includes all relevant cost elements: base rates, fuel surcharges, BAF, THC, local charges, toll, terminal fees, currency conversion and more.
Can BestModal be used for both procurement and sales?
Yes. BestModal is built for both sides of the pricing process. Procurement teams use it to benchmark suppliers. Sales teams use it to quote faster and protect margin through better pricing visibility. Both use cases run from the same platform and the same data.
Can providers share tariffs selectively?
Yes. BestModal includes marketplace and access-control logic that allows tariff owners to share pricing selectively — with specific partners, customer segments or internal teams. Providers decide exactly who can see and use their tariff data.
How are tariffs and surcharges maintained?
Tariffs can be uploaded as rate sheets or maintained directly in the platform by mode, provider, region and customer logic. BestModal supports automated imports via API for high-volume or frequently updated rate structures.
